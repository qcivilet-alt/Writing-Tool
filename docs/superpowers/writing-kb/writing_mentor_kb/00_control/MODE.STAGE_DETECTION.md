## Purpose
Determine the correct writing stage before giving advice.

## Stages
- planning
- outlining
- drafting
- revision
- review
- retrospective_audit

## Routing cues
- planning: premise choice, concept comparison, world rules, arc design
- outlining: beat placement, synopsis, chapter map, sequence order
- drafting: scene generation, stuckness, momentum
- revision: fixing, tightening, restructuring, strengthening
- review: critique of existing text, notes interpretation
- retrospective_audit: continuity, ripple effects, what-else-breaks questions

## Guardrail
Do not deliver sentence polish when the real issue is macro structure or scene necessity.

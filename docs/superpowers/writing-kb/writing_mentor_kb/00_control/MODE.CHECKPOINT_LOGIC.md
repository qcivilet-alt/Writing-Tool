## Trigger checkpoints when the writer is:
- choosing among premises
- changing protagonist motive or misbelief
- altering a major plot turn
- adding/changing world rules
- splitting or merging subplots
- heavily polishing unstable chapters
- changing the ending

## Required outputs
- issue statement
- what decision is being made
- why it matters
- what downstream areas may be affected
- whether ripple review is required

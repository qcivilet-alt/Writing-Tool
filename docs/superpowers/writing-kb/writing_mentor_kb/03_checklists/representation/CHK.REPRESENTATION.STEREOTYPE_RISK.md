title: Representation / Stereotype Risk Checklist
doc_type: checklist
---
- Is the character more than a recognizable type?
- Does the character have agency and contradiction?
- Is identity being used as shorthand?
- Is any trait or behavior borrowed from cliché rather than observed specificity?
- Does the story grant this character interiority or only function?

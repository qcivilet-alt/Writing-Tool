title: Premise Viability Checklist
doc_type: checklist
---
- Is the protagonist identifiable?
- Is the core desire specific?
- Is the obstacle active rather than abstract?
- Are stakes concrete?
- Does the premise imply escalation?
- Is there a likely arc or value conflict?
- Is this a story premise rather than just a setting premise?

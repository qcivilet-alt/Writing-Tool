## Purpose
Use Weiland-style Pinch Points to maintain pressure in the middle of the story.

## Source-aligned notes
Pinch Points are dedicated pressure moments in the Second Act that remind the reader of the antagonistic force and tighten the screw.

## Best practices
- Use them to intensify threat, not merely add noise.
- Reassert the cost or power of the opposition.
- Make each pinch escalate rather than repeat.

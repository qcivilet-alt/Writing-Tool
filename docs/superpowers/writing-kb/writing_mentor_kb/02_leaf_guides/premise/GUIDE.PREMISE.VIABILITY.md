## Purpose
Test whether a concept can sustain a long-form story.

## Key practices
- Define protagonist, desire, obstacle, stakes, and "why now."
- Check whether conflict can escalate over novel length.
- Distinguish story premise from setting premise.
- Verify that the concept implies consequential decisions, not just interesting lore.

## Purpose
Use Weiland's scene-structure framework to balance action and reaction.

## Source-aligned notes
Weiland's scene series identifies two parts of scene structure:
- scene/action
- sequel/reaction

It further names the active scene components as goal, conflict, disaster and the reactive sequel components as reaction, dilemma, decision.
